# HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 175

Introduced by: Representative Eric L. Olivarez

# RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING THE DON BOSCO HIGH SCHOOL PARAÑAQUE – ROBOTICS CLUB, FOR THEIR HISTORIC INTERNATIONAL AND NATIONAL VICTORIES IN ROBOTICS AND DIGITAL INNOVATION, INCLUDING THEIR 1ST PLACE TRIUMPH AT THE GLOBAL ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE ROBOTICS (GAR) ASIA OPEN HELD IN SINGAPORE ON JANUARY 19, 2025

WHEREAS, on January 19, 2025, Don Bosco High School Parañaque – Robotics Club made history by winning 1st Place in the Middle School Category of the Global Artificial Intelligence Robotics (GAR) Asia Open held in Singapore;

WHEREAS, the team of Josef Israel II F. Estoesta, Rian Mishka E. Sto. Domingo, and Carlos Miguel T. Sison, under the guidance of Coach Raymond B. Peñaflor, brought honor to the Philippines and to the City of Parañaque, with their coach also receiving the Excellent Coach Award;

WHEREAS, Don Bosco Robotics Club likewise achieved distinction School Category;

WHEREAS, in the Philippine Robotics Olympiad (PRO) 2024, Don Bosco students shone as Future Engineers Finalists (Eryl Samuel Partosa and Ayessah Nicole Pagao) and Future Innovators Finalists (Josef Israel Estoesta, Marvin Grafil, and Rian Mishka);

WHEREAS, the Robotics Club also excelled in the National Minecraft Education Challenge, with:

- 1st Place (JHS Group): Eryl Samuel Partosa, Lance Joven Oreta, Shina May Principe;
- 2nd Place (SHS Group): Jeremiah Lawrence Oreta, Clian Enzo Quibal, Vanessa Jhesie Ortua; 
- 2nd Place (JHS Individual): Clian Enzo Quibal; 
- 3rd Place (SHS Group): Josef Israel Estoesta and teammates;

WHEREAS, these victories were proudly celebrated during the Parañaque City IT Fair 2025, with the full support of Mayor Edwin L. Olivarez of Parañaque City and Barangay Don Bosco Chairman Mario Jimenez, alongside school officials and community partners, strengthening the journey of the Robotics Club;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the House of Representatives congratulate and commend the Don Bosco High School Parañaque – Robotics Club, their champion students, coach, school officials, and supporters, including Mayor Edwin L. Olivarez and Barangay Don Bosco Chairman Mario Jimenez, for bringing pride, honor, and recognition to the City of Parañaque and the Republic of the Philippines.

Adopted, this ___ day of ____ 2025, at the House of Representatives, Quezon City, Metro Manila.

Eric L. Olivarez
