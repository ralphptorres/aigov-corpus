Twentieth Congress First Regular Session

# Introduced by Representative RAYMOND ADRIAN SALCEDA

# RESOLUTION URGINGTHE TECHNICAL EDUCATION AND SKILLS DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY(TESDA) TO SUPPORT THE ESTABLISHMENT OF AN ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE READINESS INSTITUTE IN POLANGUI, ALBAY

WHEREAS, the Fourth Industrial Revolution, characterized by rapid advancements in digitalization, automation,and Artificial Intelligence (AI),is expected to reshape global economic systems and labor markets, with the Philippines needing to prepare its workforce for the opportunities and disruptions brought about by these technologies;

WHEREAS, Artificial Intelligence applications now influence multiple fields,including business process outsourcing, manufacturing, agriculture, logistics, healthcare, education, and government service delivery, making it imperative for the country to strengthen human capital development in AI readiness;

WHEREAS, the Technical Education and Skils Development Authority (TESDA)，as mandated under Republic Act No.7796 or the “TESDA Act of 1994,” is tasked with promoting and strengthening the quality and accessibility of technical education and skils development to prepare Filipino workers for domestic and international employment;

WHEREAS,TESDA has the institutional capacity to develop training regulations,accredit AI-related programs, certify skills, and provide the necessary technological support to ensure that Filipinos are not left behind in the global shift towards digital economies;

WHEREAS, the Local Govermment Unit (LGU) of Polangui, Albay has expressed its eager willingness to host and provide the necessary facilities for the establishment of an Artificial Inteligence Readiness Institute, demonstrating strong local government commitment and readiness to partner with national agencies for the initiative;

WHEREAS,Albay,as a strategic hub in the Bicol Region with strong academic institutions and an emerging innovation ecosystem,is well-positioned to serve as a pilot site for AI readiness programs that can later be replicated nationwide;

WHEREAS, the establishment of an AI Readiness Institute in Polangui would provide early training opportunities for students, teachers,workers，and micro， small，and medium enterprises (MSMEs),while also addressing the demand for digital upskilling in rural and regional communities;

WHEREAS, this initiative will not only advance digital literacy but will also promote inclusive growth by decentralizing access to high-technology education and by positioning Bicol as a future hub of AI-driven development;

NOW, THEREFORE,BE IT RESOLVED, that the House of Representatives urge the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) to provide full support for the establishment of an Artificial Intelligence Readiness Institute in Polangui, Albay,including but not limited to:

1. The provision of technical assistance in curriculum design and competency standards for AI-related skills training.   
2. The development and accreditation of training programs that meet international benchmarks for digital and AI competencies.   
3. The deployment of necessary technology, equipment, and infrastructure to enable AIfocused learning laboratories.   
4. The certification of trainers and assessors to ensure quality delivery of AI-related skills development programs.   
5. The establishment of partnerships with universities, industry stakeholders, and international institutions to support research, innovation, and workforce upskilling.

RESOLVED FURTHER, that copies of this Resolution be furnished to the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA), the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI),the Department of Information and Communications Technology (DICT),and the Local Government Unit of Polangui, Albay for their information and appropriate action.
