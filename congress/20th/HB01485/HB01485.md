# EXPLANATORY NOTE

The Philippine Innovation Act (RA 11293） lays down a comprehensive framework for innovation across sectors, but it does not explicitly recognize the role of artificial intelligence (AI) and data science in public sector governance. Given rapid digital transformation and the increasing demand for smarter, faster, and more responsive government services, there is a clear need to institutionalize data and AIdriven solutions as a core innovation priority.

This bill amends RA 11293 to explicitly include AI-enabled public sector innovation among the strategic priorities of the National Innovation Council, and authorizes partnerships between government, academia, and the private sector to advance this agenda.

This amendment allows existing structures under RA 11293--particularly the Innovation Fund and the National Innovation Strategy—to support AI-driven pilot programs within national and local govermment units. It is a fiscally prudent, politically viable, and operationally aligned approach to accelerating digital transformation.

Immediate passage of tis billis terefore respectfully sought.

# AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE-ENABLED PUBLIC SECTOR INNOVATION AS A STRATEGIC PRIORITY UNDER REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11293,OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE “PHILIPPINE INNOVATION ACT," AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES

Beit enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives ofthe Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION1. Section 7 of Republic Act No. 11293 is hereby amended to include a new subsection (o) to read as follows,renumbering the next subsection:

(o) Promote and fund artificial intelligence (AI)-enabled innovation in public sector governance and service delivery, including the use of data analytics, digital twins, natural language processing,and Al-powered decision-makingtoolsthroughgovernment-academe-industry collaboration; and

(p) Perform other functions as may be necessary for the implementation of this Act.

SECTION 2. Section 21 of Republic Act No. 11293 is hereby amended to include the following paragraph:

The Innovation Fund may also be used to support artificial intelligence (AI)-enabled public sector innovation projects, including those that utilize data analytics,digital twins,natural language processing,and Al-powered decision-making tools. These projects must demonstrate potential for improving public service delivery,enhancing governance, or supporting policy innovation, and shall be implemented through partnerships between government agencies, academic institutions, and private sector entities.

SECTION 3. The National Economic and Development Authority (NEDA), in coordination with the Department of Science and Technology (DOST） and other relevant agencies, shall revise or supplement the Implementing Rules and Regulations of RA 11293 to reflect the amendments under this Act within ninety (90) days from its effectivity.

SECTION 4. If any provision of this Act is declared unconstitutional, the remainder of the Act or the provision not otherwise affected shal remain in full force and effect.

SECTION 5.Allaws, decrees, executive orders, and rules and regulations inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

SECTION 6. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.

Approved.
